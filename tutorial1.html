Computer Engineering

It is a four years (eight semester) full-time undergraduate program. Out of eight semesters, seven semesters are dedicated to in house study involving course work and projects. Final semester is dedicated to Industrial Project. Students spend entire eighth semester in an IT company and do a project.The curriculum is designed by keeping industrial and research needs, as well as the rapid development in the IT industry. Flexibility in curriculum is provided through Electives, Seminars and Projects. The program is supported by well-equipped laboratories consisting of the state-of-the-art servers & computers.There are well qualified and experienced academic staffs. Almost all of them are actively involved in research

Mechanical Engineering

The aim of the mechanical engineering is to create a flexible undergraduate educational experience in design, mathematics, modeling, computing, management, engineering science, humanities and social sciences. The students completing their B.Tech course are fully prepared for technological career in industry as well in further post-graduate or management courses. The students can also utilize these interdisciplinary programs as an initiation for professional careers in business or public service sectors.

Civil Engineering

The objective of the B.Tech graduate program in Civil Engineering is to give the understudies great comprehension of civil engineering arrangements in a global, societal and ecological setting, steady with the standards of sustainable improvement. The course will make the understudies capable Identifying, planning and taking care of civil engineering problems that meet indicated execution, cost, time, well being and other quality needs and objectives with proficient and moral responsibility.
